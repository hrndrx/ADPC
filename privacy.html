Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 24, 2025

Who we are

The African Data Privacy Consortium ("ADPC", "we", "us") advocates, champions, and facilitates data privacy across the African continent and beyond.

What we collect

  • Contact form: full name, email, city (and any message you include).
  • Membership form: full name, email, organization, role, country, city, and membership type.
  • Basic usage information for site performance and security.

How we use your information

  • Respond to your inquiries and requests.
  • Process membership applications and communicate about ADPC programs.
  • Improve site content, accessibility, and security.

Legal basis

We process personal data with your consent and for our legitimate interests in running and improving ADPC and its initiatives.

Sharing

We do not sell your data. We may use service providers (e.g., form handling via Formspree) under appropriate data protection terms.

Data retention

We retain personal data only as long as needed for the purposes described or as required by law, then delete or anonymize it.

Your rights

  • Access, correct, or delete your data.
  • Withdraw consent where processing is based on consent.
  • Object to certain processing and request portability, where applicable.

To exercise rights, contact us using the contact options on the site.

Security

We apply reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational measures to protect personal data.

International transfers

Where data is transferred across borders, we take appropriate measures consistent with applicable laws.

Contact

Use the "Contact" option on the site to reach us regarding privacy matters.
