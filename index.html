The African Data Privacy Consortium (ADPC) is dedicated to advocating, championing, and facilitating data privacy for stakeholders across the African continent and beyond.

Our Mission & Vision

Building a privacy-first future for Africa through advocacy, education, and collaboration.

Our Mission

To advocate, champion, and facilitate data privacy for stakeholders across the African continent and beyond. We work tirelessly to ensure that every individual and organization has the tools, knowledge, and rights necessary to protect their digital privacy in an increasingly connected world.

Our Vision

A future where data privacy is not a luxury but a fundamental right across Africa. We envision a continent where individuals, businesses, and governments work together to create a secure, privacy-respecting digital ecosystem that drives innovation while protecting personal freedoms.

The African Data Privacy Consortium is committed to fostering an inclusive, respectful, and collaborative environment for all members. We believe that diversity of thought, background, and experience strengthens our mission and enhances our collective impact across the continent.

All members are expected to uphold these principles in their interactions within the consortium and in their professional activities related to data privacy.
